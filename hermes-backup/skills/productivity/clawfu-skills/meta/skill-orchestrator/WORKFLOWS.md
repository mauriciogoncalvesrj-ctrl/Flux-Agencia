# Pre-Built Workflows

Tested skill sequences for common challenges. Each workflow lists skills in order with purpose and handoff notes.

## Product Launch

**Use when:** Launching a new product, feature, or service
**Skills:** 8 | **Time:** 4-6 hours

```yaml
steps:
  1. first-principles: Challenge assumptions about your market
  2. inversion: Identify failure modes and anti-goals
  3. buyer-personas: Define behavior-based personas with JTBD
     input: Use first-principles insights to challenge demographic assumptions
  4. positioning: Differentiate your offering
     input: Personas → competitive alternatives
  5. grand-slam-offers: Create irresistible offer structure
     input: Positioning → value stack
  6. pre-mortem: Stress-test the launch plan
  7. launch-formula: Sequence pre-launch, launch, post-launch
  8. six-thinking-hats: Final multi-perspective go/no-go
```

## Customer Validation (YC-Style)

**Use when:** Validating a business idea before building
**Skills:** 5-6 | **Time:** 2-3 hours prep + interview time

```yaml
steps:
  1. jobs-to-be-done: Understand what progress customers seek
  2. persona-generator: Create hypothesis personas from JTBD segments
  3. mom-test: Prepare non-leading interview questions per persona
  4. objection-mapping: Anticipate purchase barriers from JTBD anxieties
  5. lean-canvas: Document hypotheses with riskiest assumptions flagged
  # After interviews:
  6. pricing-validation: Test willingness to pay using interview insights
```

## Content Strategy

**Use when:** Planning a content marketing initiative
**Skills:** 6 | **Time:** 3-4 hours

```yaml
steps:
  1. audience-research: Understand audience pain points and questions
  2. content-strategy: Plan pillars and calendar matched to audience
  3. copywriting-schwartz: Segment content by awareness level
  4. headline-formulas: Create 5-10 headlines per piece
  5. copy-frameworks: Select AIDA, PAS, etc. per content piece
  6. cta-writing: Write CTAs matched to content goals
```

## Competitive Positioning

**Use when:** Entering a crowded market or repositioning
**Skills:** 5 | **Time:** 2-3 hours

```yaml
steps:
  1. competitive-analysis: Map landscape → competitor matrix
  2. first-principles: Challenge category conventions
  3. category-design: Evaluate creating a new category
  4. positioning: Define unique position using competitive gaps
  5. value-proposition-canvas: Align position with customer needs
```

## Sales Enablement

**Use when:** Preparing sales team for a new product/market
**Skills:** 5 | **Time:** 3-4 hours

```yaml
steps:
  1. persona-generator: Buyer personas with decision criteria
  2. objection-mapping: Complete objection map with responses
  3. sales-pitch-dunford: Structure the sales conversation
  4. positioning: Competitive differentiation for sales
  5. grand-slam-offers: Offer structure with value stack
```

## Decision Making

**Use when:** Making a major strategic decision
**Skills:** 5 | **Time:** 2 hours

```yaml
steps:
  1. first-principles: Challenge assumptions about the decision
  2. inversion: Think about what could go wrong
  3. pre-mortem: Imagine failure and prevent it
  4. six-thinking-hats: Examine from all perspectives
  5. eisenhower-matrix: Prioritize resulting actions
```

## AI Video Production

**Use when:** Creating video content using AI tools
**Skills:** 5 | **Time:** 4-8 hours
**Source:** PJ Ace method

```yaml
steps:
  1. ai-video-concept: Develop idea and structured script (8-sec scenes)
  2. ai-storyboard-2x2: Create visual storyboard with 2x2 grid
     input: Script scenes from step 1
  3. ai-video-prompting: Generate prompts for Veo/Runway/Kling/Pika
     input: Storyboard frames
  4. ai-voice-design: Generate AI voice narration (ElevenLabs/Qwen3-TTS)
     input: Script dialogue
  5. ai-video-qa: Quality assurance before publication
     input: Assembled video draft
```

**When NOT to use:** Heritage brands, content requiring photorealism of real people, complex hand interactions, subtle human expressions.
